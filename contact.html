How to find me

The clinic is conveniantly located just south of the city centre. It is also very accesable from Old Trafford, Salford Quays, Chorlton and Didsbury.

By car

If arriving by car, parking is free inside the Hulme High Street Carpark, a 200 total space carpark attached to the leisure centre. The carpark may be accessed via Pennymead Close.

By tram

The closest metrolink tram stop is Deansgate-Castlefield (23 minutes walk away).

By train

The closest train stations are Deansgate-Castlefield and Oxford Road.

By bike

Bike storage for 25 bikes is available at the leisure centre.
